D10 · Build the Customer Master Report — Part 1 (Cols 1–15)

Estimated time: ~3 hrs

Today's GoalBuild the first 15 columns yourself. No copying the final query — build it section by section.
  • Cols 1–3: CustomerCode, OutletCode, Barcode — directly on Customer / CustomerOutlet
  • Col 4: CustomerType — CustomerTypeID → CustomerType → CustomerTypeLanguage
  • Cols 5–6: OrganizationCode, OrganizationName — via Route chain → Territory → Organization
  • Col 7: TerritoryCode — same Route chain
  • Col 8: PriceListCode — CustomerOutletGroup → GroupPrice → PriceList
  • Cols 9–11: Phone, Fax, Email — directly on CustomerOutlet
  • Cols 12–13: CreditLimit, Balance — AccountCustOut → Account
  • Cols 14–15: PaymentTerms, BillsNo — Customer → PaymentTerm
Starting skeleton — uncomment one section at a time, add JOINs, run, verify
